Julia Louis-Dreyfus

Biography

Julia Louis-Dreyfus was born in New York City on January 13, 1961 in New York City. Growing up, Julia lived in different places; she graduated from an all-girls' school in Maryland. Julia attended Northwestern University; she studied theater and performed with comedy groups. At 21, she dropped out of college to join the cast of Saturday Night Live. She is married and has two sons.

In addition to her work in TV and movies, Julia has been an outspoken Democrat, endorsing each Democratic nominee for President since Al Gore in 2000. Julia has voiced concern for several environmental issues, including the Keystone XL pipeline; she has also published information about voting by mail. As the daughter of a French-born immigrant, Julia has publicly spoken out against travel bans.

Known For

Julia has acted in many TV shows, including:

  • Seinfeld
  • Saturday Night Live
  • Veep
  • The New Adventures of Old Christine

Julia has also appeared in many other TV series and movies, including Arrested Development and Pixar's A Bug's Life. Julia has been active in the media since 1982. Most of her work has been in comedy, playing characters who are fiesty and sarcastic.

In regards to her preference for comedy, Julia herself has said,

Whenever I felt down, my mom would remind me that sense of humor gets you through just about anything.

and,

I'm very grateful to have the opportunity to make people laugh. It's a joyful way to make a living.

Achievements

Julia has won 7 Primetime Emmys for her roles as as a lead comedy actress in The New Adventures of Old Christine and Veep. She has also won an Emmy as a supporting actress for her role as Elaine on Seinfeld. Julia has also won 1 Golden Globe Award and 9 Screen Actors Guild awards. Upon her induction to the cast of Saturday Night Live, she was the youngest female cast member in the show's history.

In addition to her success as an actress, Julia has also won several awards as a producer. She has received 3 Emmys as a producer for her work on Veep.
