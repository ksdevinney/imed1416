What shows has she produced?
She is credited as an executive producer for Veep, as well as a producer on Watching Ellie and The New Adventures of Old Christine.
What characters has she played on SNL?

Is she married?

Who are her role models?
She has stated that some of her acting role models are Lucille Ball, Mary Tyler Moore, Madeline Kahn, Teri Garr, Valerie Harper, and Cloris Leachman.
What projects is she working on now?
Currently, she is working with Apple TV+ to develop new projects as both an executive producer and star.
